Wo Am I?

My name is Adrienne and I am a music and tech enthusiast, currently working in the healthcare industry, specifically in health information technology. I have been working full-time in the field for about 11 years now. I have two degrees, a bachelor of arts in Psychology and an Associate in Applied Science in Health Information Technology.

I come from a very musically inclined family. Music is a passion of mine. I sang in choir for 12 years as a child growing up, and in high school, and joined a few smaller ensemble groups in college. I took voice lessons for 2 years as well in high school. I am 39 years old now and recently joined a women's community choir last winter, and continue to sing in it this spring.
